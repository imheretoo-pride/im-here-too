I’m Here Too

A message of visibility, resistance, and care.

In a world that keeps trying to make us disappear, sometimes the most powerful thing you can say is: I’m here too.

Not angrier. Just true. This space exists to remind you, and everyone else, that you're not alone. We’re still here. Still proud. Still choosing joy. Still refusing to disappear.

💜 Why This Matters

Queer visibility is shrinking. Pride is being erased, co-opted, ignored. Public support is quieter. And fear is louder.

But we’ve come too far to go silent now. We’ve survived shame. We’ve broken cycles. We’ve made space to live and love as ourselves. We don’t owe the world silence just to feel safe. We’re not going back into the closet. And we won’t pretend we don’t exist.

🌈 What This Is

This is a flag in the ground. A signal flare for those looking for a light in the dark. A quiet but undeniable declaration: I’m still here. I see you. You’re not alone.

Whether you’re queer, trans, questioning, closeted, or an ally trying to show up, this is your invitation. You don’t need permission to be visible. You just need to know someone’s with you. This is that reminder.

✊ How You Can Show Up

  • ✅ Download and share the “I’m here too” and “Not Going Back” graphics. Stick them up. Wear them. Post them.
  • ✅ Tell your story. Visibility creates space. You never know who needs to hear it.
  • ✅ Support LGBTQ+ spaces, especially those led by marginalized groups.
  • ✅ Push back. Against hate. Against silence. Against the idea that this fight is over.

💬 What This Is Really About

This isn’t “us versus them.” That idea is a distraction. It’s used to divide us. It’s used to dehumanize.

This is about care. Radical, everyday, person-to-person care. Because corporations and governments don’t keep us safe. We do that for each other.

🖤 I’m Here Too

And I know you are, too. Show up. Stand proud. Be seen. Let someone know they’re not alone.
